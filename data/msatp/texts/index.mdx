<p>
  The Myanmar-Singapore Archaeology Training-Research Project (MSATP) is a

<ol style="list-style-type:lower-alpha;">
  <li>
    to carry out a joint research project in history and archaeology between

  </li>
  <li>
    to train Myanmar archaeologists and historians, including research
    specialists and teachers, in undertaking joint research projects,
  </li>
  <li>
    to disseminate information on Myanmar history and archaeology through
    publications, conferences, and symposia,
  </li>
  <li>
    to build the capacity of Myanmar institutions and individuals to conduct
    research, create publications, and lead research projects comprising both
    local and foreign participants.
